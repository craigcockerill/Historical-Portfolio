Passion is an understatement.

I'm a UI/UX designer & front-end engineer living in bright & sunny Palo Alto, CA with my fiancé and our two children cats.

I've had an interest in design and technology from a very young age. I remember watching a documentary when I was twelve about Jobs, Wozniak, Bushnell, & Gates and blindly trying to teach myself BASIC. Later in grade school I would build (admittedly terrible) websites for various classes — this was the beginning of an endless fascination. A decade later I graduated with a B.S. in Graphic Design.

It was during that time I would begin mocking my work in code as a means to an end — though the more I learned, the more I found joy bringing my work to life. I worked closely with a member of my faculty who was a designer turned developer to foster my skillset. Post graduation I immediately started designing and building websites for a wide clientele.

Now I live and work in the Bay Area and couldn't be happier. Aside from design I deep love of music, hi-fi audio, vinyl, gaming, furniture, biking. More often than not I can be caught spending time with my fiancé and our two cats.
